Who We Are

Not Born Into It.
Built For It.

The Black Sheep is an emerging band based in Pune, rapidly making a name for itself in the city's live music scene.

Our band is a collaboration of professional musicians, each with over five years of experience in performing arts and more than a decade of musical training.

We specialise in a diverse range of genres including Bollywood Rock, Progressive Rock, Classical, Semi-Classical, Commercial Bollywood, and Regional Folk Music. Our versatility allows us to captivate audiences with a blend of traditional roots and contemporary flair.

The Name

Why
Black Sheep?

"We forge our own path, striving to make a name for ourselves through sheer talent, hard work, and dedication."

Each member chose the unconventional route of becoming a musician, diverging from the traditional or expected career paths. With no industry connections or godfathers, we are not born into a lineage of musicians or entertainers.

Being the "Black Sheep" means embracing the challenges and uniqueness of not fitting into a predefined mould. It means standing out, taking risks, and daring to be different. We wear the title with pride — not as a mark of shame, but as a badge of honour.
